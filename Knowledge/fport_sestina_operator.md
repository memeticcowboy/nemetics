### Rotation Pattern

**Sestina end-word rotation (spiral retrogression):**
```
Stanza 1: A B C D E F
Stanza 2: F A E B D C
Stanza 3: C F D A B E
...
```

- Force each term into all 6 line positions (context variation)
- Create unexpected adjacencies (B follows A in stanza 2, but D follows A in stanza 4)
- Reveal which transitions feel natural vs forced

**Key insight:** When forced transition feels unnatural → **missing functor lives in that gap**
