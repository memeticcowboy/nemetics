# Human Augmentation

**Technologies enhancing cognitive abilities**—brain-computer interfaces, nootropics, neurostimulation—expanding memory, learning speed, and problem-solving beyond evolutionary constraints. Raises profound questions about authenticity, equity, and the boundary between therapy and enhancement.

## The Authenticity Question

Where does the natural self end and the augmented self begin? When enhancement becomes essential to identity, the boundary dissolves into posthuman possibility.
