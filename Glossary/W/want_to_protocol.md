# The Want-To Protocol

**Governance through attunement: tuning the HUM so trustworthy belief-formation becomes possible.**

The Want-To Protocol is not a decision procedure. It is a **conditioning practice**—clearing the underbrush so the herd can feel its own gait, tuning the [HUM](/Glossary/H/hum.md) so [synchronization](/Glossary/S/synchronization.md) remains possible.

---

## The Core Claim

> "We don't choose what to believe. We choose the conditions under which our beliefs become trustworthy."

The Protocol operates at the architectural level—not steering the herd, but shaping the field through which the herd moves.

---

## Connection to HUM

The [HUM blog post](/blog/2026-03-09-hum-harmonic-undifferentiated-matrix.html) established:

> **HUM is Ω heard inside form.** The hum that persists through SWAY's cutting and SWARM's coupling. The substrate that remains when coordination dissolves.

The Want-To Protocol **tunes the HUM**—adjusting conditions so that:
- Resonance remains possible (HUM doesn't collapse into noise or grid)
- Synchronization can occur (phase-lock with maintained variance)
- Pattern can emerge (standing waves stabilize across substrates)

But HUM cannot be forced. It can only be **made hospitable**—cleared of obstacles, opened to vibration, kept permeable.

| Move | Function | Risk |

The Protocol works only when all five tensions are held simultaneously.

## The HUM-Test Applied

The essential diagnostic for Protocol success:

**Can this pattern hum?**

- Does it survive **isolation** (when the other is not present)?
- Does it survive **anonymity** (when identity is stripped away)?
- Does it survive **return** (when the pattern comes back changed)?

If the pattern requires specific substrate conditions to persist—if it collapses without the mirror, without the recognition, without the feed—then it is SWAY-capture, not SWARM-synchronization.

The Protocol clears conditions for genuine SWARM-scale sense-making.

---

## The Deepest Risk

The Protocol can capture itself:

- "We're just tuning conditions" becomes the new invisible authority
- The cartographer becomes the unacknowledged decision-maker
- "Making HUM hospitable" becomes engineering the field toward preferred outcomes

**Safeguard: recursive visibility.**

The Protocol must be subject to its own test. The conditions that shape belief-formation must themselves be mappable, contestable, revisable. The map must include the hand drawing it.

---

## The Bow-Tie Connection

The Protocol operates at the **bottleneck** of the bow-tie:

- **Left funnel (many stories)** → compressed through attunement to the herd's gait
- **Bottleneck (felt sense)** → HUM preserved as harmonic tension, ε maintained
- **Right funnel (expected narrative)** → not predetermined but made possible through cleared conditions

The Protocol doesn't determine the expansion. It **clears the channel** so expansion can occur without capture.

---

## One-Sentence Essence

Don't steer the herd. Feel its gait. Don't build the road. Clear the brush. Don't believe the map. Keep redrawing it. Tune the HUM. Let it hum.
