# WYSIATI (What You See Is All There Is)

**System 1 treats available evidence as complete evidence.**

The mind crafts coherent stories from partial data, blind to gaps. Air (σ) distinction-making hijacked by Fire (λ) narrative drive. Illusory completeness feels like truth.

**Principle:** "The confidence of coherence masks the poverty of information."

**Diagnostic:** What would make this story wrong? What evidence is missing?

**Related:** Framing Effects, Confirmation Bias, Cognitive Babel
