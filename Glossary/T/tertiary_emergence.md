# Tertiary Emergence

**The process by which third-order patterns arise from tension between first and second, without synthesis.**

Not compromise. Generation from beneath (Ω-layer). Z-operator action: coordination that creates. Requires holding tension without resolution.

**Formula:** `Tertiary = Z(A, B) + ε` where Z is not function but generative coordination

**Diagnostic:** Does the emergence resolve the tension or hold it productively?
