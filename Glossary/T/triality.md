# Triality

**Avoidance of dualistic oppositions like thesis/antithesis or self/other.**

Third position is not synthesis but emergence from beneath. Z-operator action: coordination that generates. Ω-layer differentiation. Refuses closure of binary resolution.

**Principle:** "Not A or B, not A and B, but C that makes A and B possible."

**Diagnostic:** Does the "third" option resolve the tension or hold it productively?
