# ε (Epsilon)

**Essential, irreducible uncertainty preserved at every level of the stack.**

ε ≠ 0 is not a limitation to be overcome. It is the thermodynamic condition that prevents a sensemaking system from collapsing into a MemeGrid. Where ε is maintained, threads remain revisable, knots remain untie-able, and the bow-tie remembers that its bottleneck is forgetting, not truth.

**Principle:** "ε is the gap that keeps the system alive."

**Diagnostic:** Does the system claim ε = 0? Does it present its compressions as complete? If so, MemeGrid conditions are forming.

**What It Is NOT:**
- Not error or noise (it is structurally necessary)
- Not ignorance to be remedied (it is the condition of health)
- Not vagueness (it is precise about what cannot be made precise)

**Failure Mode:** Claiming ε = 0—presenting a compression as if nothing was lost, a distinction as if nothing was excluded, a coordination as if no alternatives exist.
