# Social Identity Bias

**Ingroup favoritism and outgroup discrimination in AI and humans.**

LLMs show 93% positive bias for "We are" vs 115% negative for "They are." Water (ρ) resonance with ingroup; Metal (μ) boundary enforcement between groups. AI amplifies polarization without social friction that moderates human prejudice.

**Principle:** "The tribe that thinks together, sinks together."

**Diagnostic:** Would the same statement be evaluated differently from ingroup vs outgroup source?
