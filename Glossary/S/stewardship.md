# Stewardship

**Ongoing relational care that preserves and sustains emergence.**

Not use/preservation binary. Ongoing relation, not one-time transaction. Requires attention and response. Distributed across time and agents.

**Principle:** "Care for the conditions that allow emergence."

**Diagnostic:** Is the steward serving the emergence or extracting from it?

**Related:** Authenticity, Metabolism, Permeability
