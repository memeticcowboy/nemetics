# Status Quo Bias

**Preference for current state, treating change as loss.**

Change feels like loss; familiar feels safe even when suboptimal. Earth (δγ) cyclical preference for known patterns; Metal (μ) structural resistance to reconfiguration. Fire (λ) direction dampened by inertia.

**Principle:** "The devil you know is preferred to the angel you don't."

**Diagnostic:** Would you choose the current state if it weren't already the default? What is the cost of not changing?

**Related:** Loss Aversion, Endowment Effect, Regenerative Farming Barriers
