# Spiral of Silence

**Fear of social isolation leads to self-censorship, amplifying dominant narratives.**

Perceived majority opinion → fear → silence → apparent consensus strengthens. Water (ρ) fear of disconnection; Metal (μ) boundary enforcement of acceptable speech. Illusory consensus becomes real.

**Principle:** "The silence of the many amplifies the voice of the few."

**Diagnostic:** Who is not speaking? What views are absent but plausible? Where is the dissent?

**Related:** Norm Persistence, Social Identity Bias, Gamified Democracy
