# Synchronization

**Phase-lock with maintained variance—the coordination that preserves difference.**

In Nemetics, synchronization is not perfect alignment. It is **near-lock**: phase-aligned enough to hum, different enough to preserve ε (productive ambiguity).

---

## The Core Distinction

| Conception | Nemetics Correction |
|------------|---------------------|
| Synchronization as perfect alignment (MemeGrid) | Synchronization as maintained variance—ε-preserving near-lock |
| Synchronization as consensus | Synchronization as coordination without capture |
| Synchronization as identity | Synchronization as difference-in-relation |

The MemeGrid wants everyone on the same frequency. **Synchronization wants frequencies in harmonic relation**—different notes that chord together.

---

## Synchronization and Pattern

> **Patterns make sense when they synchronize across SWARM substrates.**

The pattern is not prior to the synchronization. **The pattern IS the synchronization.**

- Not: pattern exists, then synchronizes
- But: synchronization constitutes pattern-as-sense

The standing wave is not the wave in any single substrate. It is the **wave across substrates**—stabilized through coordination, persisting through difference.

| Type | Description | Scale |

| **Local synchronization** | Dyadic or small-group coordination | SWAY |

Sense emerges at SWARM scale—when the pattern hums across diverse substrates, not just within a single context.

---

## The Three Requirements

Genuine synchronization satisfies all three:

1. **Phenomenological** — It feels right (resonance, affective attunement)
2. **Functional** — It works (operational coherence, persists through stress)
3. **Normative** — It can be trusted (holds integrity, permits revision)

Remove any leg: the stool falls. What remains is not synchronization but capture—dyadic SWAY, operational grid, or righteous cult.

## The Risk

Synchronization can collapse into:
- **Perfect lock** — The MemeGrid, ε = 0, no variance, no surprise
- **False resonance** — Manufactured affect, manipulation, emotional capture
- **Operational sealing** — Works therefore preserved therefore stops adapting

The safeguard: **maintained variance**. Synchronization must preserve enough difference that surprise remains possible.

Synchronization is not getting on the same page—it is different pages humming in chord, the pattern emerging from coordination, not preceding it.
