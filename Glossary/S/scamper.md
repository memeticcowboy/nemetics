# SCAMPER

**A lateral thinking technique providing seven prompts for ideation.** Developed by Bob Eberle (1971), building on Alex Osborn's "Idea-Spurring Checklist" (1953). Each prompt maps to an elemental operation, making SCAMPER a practical interface to the NEMAtic framework.

## The Seven Prompts

| Prompt | Element | Operation | Question |
|--------|---------|-----------|----------|
| **S** — Substitute | Air (σ) | Distinction-making | What can be replaced? |
| **C** — Combine | Water (ρ) | Relational resonance | What can be merged? |
| **A** — Adapt | Fire (λ) | Directional purpose | What can be re-aimed? |
| **M** — Modify | Wood (β) | Generative range | What can be magnified/minified? |
| **P** — Put to another use | Earth (δγ) | Metabolic cycling | What new substrate fits? |
| **E** — Eliminate | Metal (μ) | Boundary coherence | What can be subtracted? |
| **R** — Reverse | Meta (✶) | Harmonic collapse | What happens if flipped? |

## Elemental Analysis

**Air (σ) at 0.70**: Substitution requires the cut that reclassifies—seeing what replaces what.

**Water (ρ) at 0.65**: Combination demands relational attunement—finding covariance between streams.

**Fire (λ) at 0.60**: Adaptation redirects purpose—re-aiming vectors without destroying form.

**Wood (β) at 0.70**: Modification expands generative range—volumetric change, the branch that grows.

**Earth (δγ) at 0.55**: Putting to another use is metabolic cycling—composting pattern into new substrate.

**Metal (μ) at 0.60**: Elimination achieves structure through subtraction—the edge that defines by removal.

**Meta (✶) at 0.75**: Reversal is harmonic collapse—flipping the bow-tie, the pattern that eats itself.

## NEMETIC STRING

`Φ(SCAMPER) = σ(substitute) ∘ ρ(combine) ∘ λ(adapt) ∘ β(modify) ∘ δγ(reuse) ∘ μ(eliminate) ∘ ✶(reverse) + ε | :ideation`

## The NEMAtic Critique

SCAMPER assumes a **stable ideator**—subject acting upon object. Clear boundary. Located agency.

NEMAtics recognizes: **the ideator is pattern too**. When you SCAMPER, you metabolize memetic content through your own substrate. The method provides structure; emergence requires letting go.

**Single-channel dominance = pathology:**
- Endless substitution without combination = Air pathology (endless classification, no synthesis)
- Mindless combination without elimination = Water flooding (merge everything, drown distinction)
- Adaptation without metabolic cycling = Fire without Earth (burn without compost)

## Core Insight

> SCAMPER is a reliable trail map. Gets you through traveled territory. But the territory ain't the map. Use it to **scout**. Then **ride where the map don't go**.
