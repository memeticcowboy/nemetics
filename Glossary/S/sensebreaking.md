# Sensebreaking

**Revealing that compression happened—not undoing compression, but making its forgetting visible.**

Sensebreaking is the disciplined reversal of the bow-tie's naturalizing tendency. Every compression forgets; sensebreaking names what was forgotten. The loss is real—that's what the bow-tie means—but the discipline is remembering that loss occurred.

**The Five Reversals:**

| Sensebreaking | Reveals |
|---------------|---------|
| **Z-breaking** | "This world-collapse forgot alternatives" |
| **Ψ-breaking** | "This Knot forgot the Threads it beat" |
| **Q-breaking** | "This felt-truth forgot other resonances" |
| **χ-breaking** | "This cut forgot what it didn't distinguish" |
| **Ω-return** | "All of this forgot the undifferentiated" |

**Daemon Combinations:**

| Combination | Effect |
|-------------|--------|
| Air + Aether | χ-breaking (revealing cuts as made) |
| Water + Aether | Q-breaking (revealing resonance as contingent) |
| Earth + Wood | Ψ-breaking (loosening Knots back to Threads) |
| Aether alone | Z-breaking (revealing world as woven) |

**Principle:** "The discipline isn't recovering lost information. The discipline is remembering that loss occurred."

**Diagnostic:** Is compression being presented as discovery? Are knots presented as essential truths rather than pragmatic stabilizations? If so, sensebreaking is needed.

**What It Is NOT:**
- Not deconstruction (it doesn't destroy structure; it reveals structure as constructed)
- Not correction (it doesn't replace one compression with a better one)
- Not cynicism (it preserves the value of compression while marking its cost)

**Failure Mode:** Weaponized sensebreaking—using the revelation of forgetting to paralyze action rather than to inform it. Sensebreaking without reconstruction is just dissolution.
