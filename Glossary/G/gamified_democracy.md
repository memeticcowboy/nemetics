# Gamified Democracy

**Using game mechanics to incentivize civic participation.**

Points, badges, leaderboards for voting, deliberation, engagement. Wood (β) branching exploration; Fire (λ) motivation; Water (ρ) social feedback. Risks distorting democratic values when extrinsic motivation replaces intrinsic civic duty.

**Principle:** "The game that governs is not the governance that serves."

**Diagnostic:** Would participation continue without rewards? Is the game serving democracy or engagement metrics? What values are being gamed?
