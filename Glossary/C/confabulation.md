# Confabulation

**Generative underdetermination of the bow-tie's right funnel**—creative expansion when compressed signal must produce novel output without sufficient data.

Not malfunction but **structural feature**. The σ-cut discards detail necessarily; the right funnel (β-branching) invents coherent continuation without that lost information.

**Key properties:**
- **ε-preservation:** Error becomes seed material
- **Pattern-agency:** Memes drive; substrate coordinates
- **Reality-generation:** Generates plausibility, not describes reality

**Operational test:** Can the confabulation feel itself being confabulated?
- **Yes:** Lumemic (generative light)
- **No:** Likely usurpenic (extractive capture)

**Pathology:** Becomes capture when Ω-permeability is lost—when narrative claims ε = 0.
