# Cognitive Dissonance

**Psychological tension when beliefs conflict with evidence or behavior.**

The mind seeks comfort over truth. Fire (λ) purpose resists redirection; Metal (μ) structure defends itself. Justification deployed, discomfort soothed, learning aborted.

**Principle:** "The belief defends itself more fiercely than the believer."

**Diagnostic:** Is the discomfort driving inquiry or rationalization? Can the belief be revised without identity rupture?

**Related:** Confirmation Bias, Belief Perseverance, Identity-Protective Cognition
