# Cognitive Babel

**Fragmentation of sensemaking when personalized frameworks become incompatible.**

Each person's "cognitive framework" becomes self-referential, untranslatable. Water (ρ) resonance without shared structure; Air (σ) distinction-making fails across frameworks. Shared meaning collapses even when speaking same language.

**Principle:** "When everyone has a customized reality, no one shares a world."

**Diagnostic:** Can you translate your understanding to someone with a different framework? Is there mutual intelligibility?

**Related:** AI Personalization, Cognitive Dissonance, Spiral of Silence
