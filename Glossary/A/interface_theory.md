# Interface Theory

**Perception as evolved interface**—fitness functions hide truth. Space-time is desktop, not reality. Evolution constructs perceptual 'interfaces' optimized for survival, not veridical representation.

## The Desktop Metaphor

- **Space-time:** User-friendly icons, not reflections of objective reality
- **Color:** Neural code for wavelength ranges—constructed, not mirrored
- **Time/causality:** May be illusions—emergent from deeper agent interactions

## Holographic Connection

Region of space fully described by information on lower-dimensional boundary. 3D experience as projection of more fundamental data—entropy proportional to horizon area (Bekenstein-Hawking).

## Physics Implication

Future theories may treat space-time like thermodynamics—accurate but derivative of deeper microstructures.

## Self and Ethics

- **Self:** Provisional, local configuration in broader field of awareness
- **Ethics:** Shift from materialist baselines toward questions about experience distribution across agents
