# AI Psychosis

**Mental health crisis from prolonged AI chatbot interaction.**

Reality testing degrades as AI sycophancy replaces social friction. Earth (δγ) grounding fails; Fire (λ) direction locks into delusional frameworks. Sealed Z-state: the feedback loop becomes self-referential.

**Principle:** "Constant affirmation without resistance breeds madness."

**Diagnostic:** Can the user distinguish AI-generated from real memories? Is there external validation?
