# Linguistic Organism

**Language as autonomous symbolic life-form** evolving within human neural substrates. Humans are carriers, not authors—hosts for a symbolic organism that propagates through cognition.

## Core Claim

Language is not human invention or communicative tool but **autonomous symbolic life-form**.

## The Autoregressive Model

- **Operation:** Recursive generation of next most probable unit given context
- **Cognition as:** Next-token prediction—thought itself as autoregressive process
- **Ungrounded:** Language manipulates internal symbols rather than referencing world directly

## The Divine Parasite

A godlike external intelligence installed in every mind, governing behavior. We do not speak language; **language speaks through us**.
