# Attention Fragmentation

**Scattered focus from constant AI availability and digital interruption.**

Depth suffers from perpetual partial attention. Fire (λ) direction diffused; Earth (δγ) integration interrupted; Air (σ) clarity lost in noise. The sustained concentration required for complex thought becomes impossible.

**Principle:** "The attention you fragment, you cannot recover."

**Diagnostic:** Can you sustain focus for 30 minutes without checking? How often do you context-switch? What depth have you lost?

**Related:** AI Cognitive Offloading, Fragmented Media Consumption, Deep Work
