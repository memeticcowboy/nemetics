# AI Cognitive Offloading

**Delegating memory and thinking to AI systems.**

Weakening of Air (σ) distinction-making, Earth (δγ) memory integration. Metal (μ) structure outsourced. The mind atrophies what it doesn't use. Retrieval practice replaced by instant generation.

**Principle:** "The mind you don't use, you lose."

**Diagnostic:** Can you perform the task without AI? Has your baseline capability degraded? What have you stopped practicing?

**Related:** AI Psychosis, Source Monitoring Error, Attention Fragmentation
