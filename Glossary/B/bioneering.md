# Bioneering

The disciplined application of **β (Wood)** + **δγ (Earth)** operators to human socio-technical systems within the [Four Spheres](four-spheres.html) as habitat constraints.

## Core Formula

**Bioneering = (β ∘ δγ)(Systems) ⊕_spheres [γ_regen ≥ 1]**

## The Two Operators

### β (Wood): The Pioneering Aspect
- Generative range expansion into viable niches
- Branching ratio of solutions
- Finding applications of life's patterns that weren't already there

### δγ (Earth): The Bio Aspect
- Metabolic cycling—what gets decomposed, what regenerates
- γ_regen > 1 = expanding fertility
- The bioneer's questions: *What does this release? What does it regenerate?*

## Critical Distinctions

| Without | Result | Example |
|---------|--------|---------|
| **β without δγ** | Tangled chaos | Solar panels made with slave labor—green tech without ethics |
| **δγ without β** | Stagnation | Deep ecology opposing all intervention—preservation without innovation |

## Sustainability Assessment

| γ_regen | Status |
|---------|--------|
| γ < 1 | Extractive depletion (not bioneering) |
| γ ≈ 1 | Sustainable mutual aid (bioneering achieved) |
| γ >> 1 | Cancerous overgrowth (unstable) |
