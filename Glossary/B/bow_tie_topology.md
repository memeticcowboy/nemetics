# Bow-Tie Topology

**The unavoidable compression–expansion shape of sensemaking under constraint.**

Whenever inputs exceed capacity and action must occur now, a finite system must: compress (forget most distinctions), act through a bottleneck (Now), and expand creatively (invent usable output). This is not optional, not designed, not local to any single layer. It is the forced topology of meaning under finitude.

**Principle:** "The bottleneck is forgetting, not truth."

**Diagnostic:** Can participants see that compression happened? Can they name what was lost? If the bottleneck presents itself as discovery rather than forgetting, MemeGrid conditions are active.

**What It Is NOT:**
- Not a component or module (it is global and structural)
- Not one-per-daemon (one bow-tie spans the stack; daemons bias its shape)
- Not optional or designed (it is physics + time + finitude)

**Canonical Path:**
```
Ω (It-Field) → χ compression (I-Tube) → Q weighting (My-Stream) → Ψ coherence (Knot competition) → Z collapse (Now) → Expansion (creative reconstruction)
```

**Failure Mode:** A bow-tie that has forgotten it's a bow-tie. Right funnel collapsed (only one expansion permitted), Ω-return sealed, compression presented as truth. This is the structural definition of MemeGrid.
