# Barnum Effect

**Acceptance of vague, universal statements as highly accurate and personally meaningful.**

"You have a great need for other people to like and admire you" — everyone nods. Earth (δγ) cognitive ease meets Water (ρ) felt resonance. No actual information content required.

**Principle:** "The vaguer the statement, the more personal it feels."

**Diagnostic:** Does the 'insight' apply to everyone? Would you reject it if attributed to someone else?

**Related:** WYSIATI, AI Personalization, Cognitive Babel
