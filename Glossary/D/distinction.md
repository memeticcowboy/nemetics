# Distinction

**Dynamic differentiation without fixed substance or forced unity.**

σ-operator: cuts that enable, not scar tissue. Maintains ε (uncertainty) in the cut. Revisable: can be re-cut without rupture. Not separation but relational boundary.

**Principle:** "The cut that connects."

**Diagnostic:** Does the distinction open possibility or foreclose it?

**Related:** Multiplicity, ✶-State, Permeability
