# MemeGrid (▩)

**A hard global minimum in the memetic gradient field where world-level coordination collapses without Ω-permeability, eliminating all viable alternative descent paths.**

Not an ideology. Not a belief system. Not a culture. A MemeGrid is a structural condition—a topological catastrophe in which meaning ceases to evolve and becomes compulsorily reproductive. The field no longer adapts. It only reproduces.

**Operator Stack:** `Z without Ω`

**Principle:** "A bow-tie that has forgotten it's a bow-tie."

**Diagnostic markers:**
- Right funnel collapsed (only one expansion permitted)
- Ω-return sealed (no re-entry of what was lost)
- Compression presented as discovery, not as forgetting
- The bottleneck claims to be truth
- Meta-language neutralized (questioning the frame is illegitimate)

**What It Is NOT:**
- Not "too much structure" (it is structure denying its own lossiness)
- Not a content position (ideologies may live inside a MemeGrid; they are not its cause)
- Not necessarily imposed by a villain (structural incentives can produce MemeGrid without any actor intending it)

**Formation:** Z collapses absolutely; Ω is excluded, silenced, or weaponized; knots fuse; gradients flatten toward compulsory descent. Threads calcify into memeplexes. The Lattice becomes a prison rather than a scaffold.

**Failure Mode:** Total. Irreversible without rupture. Power With collapses into Power Over.

**Related:** Co-SPHERE, Ω-Permeability, Bow-Tie Topology, Soft Global Minimum, Lattice
