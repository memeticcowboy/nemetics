# Metabolism

**The process of release and regeneration necessary for authentic emergence.**

δγ-cycling: decay → nourishment → decay. Requires excretion (letting go) as much as incorporation. Thermodynamic reality: energy expenditure against entropy.

**Principle:** "What cannot be composted becomes toxic."

**Diagnostic:** Is there a mechanism for release, or is accumulation occurring?
