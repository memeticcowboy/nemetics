# Metabolic Cost

**The energetic/attentional price exacted from the engager**; the friction required to extract meaning from a pattern. The thermodynamic metric for evaluating memetic health.

## The Two Economies

| Pattern Type | Metabolic Cost | Uptake Speed | Longevity |
|--------------|----------------|--------------|-----------|
| **Lumemic** (generative) | HIGH | Slow | Deep |
| **Usurpenic** (extractive) | LOW | Fast | Shallow |

## Operational Definition

**Metabolic Cost = Attention × Time × Cognitive Effort**

- **High metabolic cost**: The reader must work, interpret, ride the trail
- **Low metabolic cost**: Pattern demands nothing, offers immediate gratification

## Elemental Analysis

**Earth (δγ) at 0.90:** The core principle—cycling, digestion, the work of extraction. What goes in must be metabolized.

**Metal (μ) at 0.60:** The edge that cuts; the resistance that generates friction.

**Fire (λ) at 0.45:** The directionality of attention; energy expenditure toward meaning.
