# Multiplicity

**Emphasis on refusal of binary capture and embracing complexity.**

More-than-two, less-than-one. Many patterns held simultaneously without single dominance. Source of creativity and adaptability. Water (ρ) resonance enables holding contradictory patterns without premature resolution.

**Principle:** "Not both/and, not neither/nor, but many-at-once."

**Diagnostic:** Does the system allow contradictory patterns to coexist without premature resolution?
