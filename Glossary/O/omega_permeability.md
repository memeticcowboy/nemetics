# Ω-Permeability

**The openness of a narrative system to revision and uncertainty**, preventing rigid or dogmatic belief.

The opposite of MemeGrid—capacity to be surprised, to adjust, to remain incomplete.

**Diagnostic markers:**
- Can the system be surprised by its own output?
- Is revision possible without rupture?
- Does the narrative claim ε = 0?

**In confabulation:** Healthy confabulation maintains Ω-permeability (felt sense of construction); captured confabulation loses it (mistakes output for ground truth).

**Principle:** "Ω-Permeability is the openness that prevents MemeGrid formation."
