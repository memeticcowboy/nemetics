# Overton Window

**Range of policies politically acceptable to mainstream at given time.**

Who defines "acceptable"? How do ideas move from unthinkable to policy? Metal (μ) boundary enforcement of discourse; Water (ρ) social normalization. Choke points where democratic discourse can be captured.

**Principle:** "The window moves not by argument but by normalization."

**Diagnostic:** Who sets the boundaries of acceptable debate? What voices are outside the window?
