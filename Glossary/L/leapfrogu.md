# LeapFrogU

An institutional regime designed to transform We-Sphere educational substrates into Threadplex coherence **without MemeGrid capture**. LeapFrogU operates through the **upload imperative** (lumemic replication) versus download pathology (usurpenic Knot formation).

## Core Mechanism

**β-branching at institutional scale**, enabling organizations to leap between attractor basins rather than following incremental reform gradients into MemeGrid capture.

| Mode | Force | Effect |
|------|-------|--------|

| **Download** (passive banking) | Usurpenic | ε constrained, Knot formation |
