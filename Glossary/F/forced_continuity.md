# Forced Continuity

**Making subscription cancellation difficult while making sign-up easy.**

Dark pattern sub-type. Metal (μ) boundary weaponized — structural enforcement of retention. The roach motel: easy in, impossible out. Water (ρ) flow interrupted at exit only.

**Principle:** "Friction asymmetrically applied is extraction, not service."

**Diagnostic:** Is exit as easy as entry? Are cancellation steps proportional to signup steps?
