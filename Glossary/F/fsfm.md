# FSFM

**Friction Suppression Failure Mode** — a governance pathology in which ethical oversight mechanisms become optimized for scalability, procedural efficiency, and institutional legitimacy until they suppress the friction required to detect and interrupt harm.

## Canonical Definition

> "FSFM is the process by which ethical governance becomes optimized for institutional smoothness rather than harm interruption."

Or tighter: **"Ethics optimized for scale instead of truth."**

## The Five-Stage Drift

| Stage | Name | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| 1 | Ethical Diagnosis | Living recognition of harm, asymmetry, risk |
| 2 | Institutional Adoption | Organizations adopt language/tools to signal responsibility |
| 3 | Friction Reduction | Ethical deliberation becomes standardized procedure |
| 4 | Automation & Formalization | Judgment becomes compliance infrastructure |
| 5 | Symbolic Success / Substantive Failure | Governance artifacts persist while harms continue |

**Short form:** Ethics → Process → Compliance → Infrastructure → Theater

## The ε → 0 Condition

When institutions optimize governance for predictability, they remove ε (productive uncertainty):

- Dissent becomes noise
- Disagreement becomes inefficiency
- Ambiguity becomes risk

**Result:** Thermodynamically closed governance—form persists, metabolism atrophies.

## The Six Diagnostic Tests

A system has entered FSFM when:

1. **Stop Power Vanishes** — No actor can halt deployment (Ω gone)
2. **Ethical Judgment Delegated** — Decisions become templates (σ severed from ✶)
3. **Conflict Converted to Consensus** — Disagreement disappears (ρ severed)
4. **Context Flattened** — Local cases become risk categories (β severed)
5. **Risk Accelerates System** — Higher stakes → faster deployment (δγ disabled)
6. **Ethics Improves Throughput** — "Responsible AI" increases efficiency *(red flag)*

## Why FSFM Is Rewarded

**FSFM feels like success:**
- Speed ↑
- Predictability ↑
- Scalability ↑
- Investor confidence ↑
- Audit success ↑

The system selects for **ethical forms that cannot interrupt power**.
