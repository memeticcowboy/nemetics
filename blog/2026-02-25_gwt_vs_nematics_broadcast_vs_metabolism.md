## Key Tensions

### 1. Access vs Transformation

- **GWT prioritizes access**: What gets into the workspace?
- **NEMAtics prioritizes transformation**: What happens during compression/expansion?

### 2. The Cartesian Residue

- **GWT's theater metaphor risks Cartesian residue**: Who watches the broadcast?
- **NEMAtic bow-tie has no audience**: Only recursive flow—no homunculus, no central observer

### 3. Algorithmic vs Ethical Competition

- **GWT competition is algorithmic**: Informational salience, neural activation
- **NEMAtic metabolism includes ethical dimension**: **Usurpenic vs. lumemic pattern assessment**—does the pattern sustain or capture?

## The Synthesis

**GWT and NEMAtics are complementary**:

- **GWT describes the mechanism**: How consciousness emerges from neural broadcast
- **NEMAtics describes the conditions**: How to maintain healthy consciousness vs. pathological capture

GWT tells us **that** the broadcast happens.

NEMAtics tells us **whether** the broadcast sustains life or becomes a trap.
