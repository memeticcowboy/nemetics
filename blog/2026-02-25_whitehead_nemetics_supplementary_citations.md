## Simple Physical Feelings and Time

The Whitehead Encyclopedia quotes Whitehead on **"simple physical feelings"**:

> "In virtue of these feelings, **time is the conformation of the immediate present to the past**." [1]

This directly supports the nemetic emphasis on **occasions metabolizing their past into their present**—time itself is this conformation, this coordination of present pattern with inherited pattern.

## The Fundamental Situation

The Whitehead Encyclopedia stresses:

> "The fundamental situation is **'an actual occasion's subjective experiencing of its causal past'** and that actual occasions are **'the reasons for time'**." [1]

There is no going "behind" occasions to some deeper substrate. The only "ground" is this **ongoing process of occasions prehending occasions**.

This supports the line about Whitehead providing a **"metaphysical ground you try not to stand on too firmly."**

## Drops of Experience

Larval Subjects provides helpful secondary commentary, glossing actual occasions as **"drops of experience"** and emphasizing Whitehead's own formulation:

> "**The many become one, and are increased by one.**" [2]

This reiterates that enduring objects are **"immense societies…of actual occasions,"** grounding the contrast between persisting units and streams of occasions.

## The Circular Structure of Prehension

The IEP gives the key line:

> "The occasion prehends past occasions as **'objective data'** and creatively combines them in its own concrescence, then **'once it has become fully actualized, the occasion as superject becomes an objective datum for those occasions which follow it, and the process begins again.'**" [3]

This **circular structure** justifies the claim that prehension is a **pattern operation that transforms both sides**:
- The **prehending occasion** is constituted by how it coordinates with the past
- The **past** is only what it is as it is taken up as "objective data" for later becomings

## The Ontological Principle

The Whitehead Encyclopedia highlights:

> "**Actual occasions are the reasons for time.**" [1]

I.e., there is no going behind occasions to some deeper substrate. This is the **nemetic ontology**: no fixed ground, only ongoing pattern coordination.

## The Nemetic Cycle

```
PAST OCCASIONS → PREHENSION → CONCRESCENCE → NEW OCCASION
                      ↑                              ↓
                      └────── OBJECTIVE DATA ────────┘
```

Each occasion:
1. **Prehends** past occasions as objective data
2. **Creatively combines** them in concrescence
3. **Becomes** fully actualized
4. **Becomes objective datum** for future occasions
5. **Process begins again**

This is **nemetic coordination as ontology**—not metaphor, not special case, but the basic structure of reality.
