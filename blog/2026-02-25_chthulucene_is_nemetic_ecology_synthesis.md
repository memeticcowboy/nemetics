## The Core Claim

As **nemetic ecology**, the **Chthulucene** is:

> **Pattern coordination across incommensurable substrates**: microbes, forests, servers, capital flows, myths, all intra-acting **without a single unit of account**.

## No Reduction, No Common Currency

There is:
- **No reduction** to a common currency (like carbon, DNA, or memes)
- **No commensuration** that would make everything comparable
- **No single metric** that could capture the whole

Instead, **patterns emerge from**:
- **Partial translations**—approximate understandings across difference
- **Frictions**—the resistances that generate new forms
- **Alliances**—temporary coordinations among heterogeneous beings [1]

## Worlding as Ongoing

"**Worlding**" is ongoing: worlds are **made and unmade** through these cross-substrate coordinations, not pre-given terrains onto which agents act.

This is the **nemetic insight**: there is no stable ground, only **continuous patterning**.

## The Phrase That Fits

Your phrase—**"pattern coordination across incommensurable substrates without reduction to common unit"**—fits Haraway precisely.

**Tentacular thinking** tracks how different kinds of lines hook into each other:
- **Ecological** lines (species, ecosystems, climates)
- **Infrastructural** lines (roads, cables, supply chains)
- **Narrative** lines (myths, histories, futures)
- **Financial** lines (markets, debts, speculative flows)
- **Microbial** lines (bacteria, fungi, viromes)

...**without ever fully commensurating**.

## The Methodological Template

Taken together, Haraway gives you a **methodological template for NEMAtic work**:

1. **Think tentacularly**—follow lines, don't seek overview
2. **Make-with**—sympoietic practice across difference
3. **Stay inside the Cthulucene's messy pattern ecology**—no stepping outside
4. **Treat knowledge as tuning and re-attaching lines**—not commanding the web from above
