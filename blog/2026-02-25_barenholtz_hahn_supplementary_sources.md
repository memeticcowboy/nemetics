## The Autoregressive Core

Across all sources, the **consistent claim**:

> Human cognition and LLM operation are **instances of the same underlying process**: autoregressive prediction of the next token given context.

The difference is not kind but **scale and substrate**:
- **LLMs**: billions of parameters, trained on text corpora
- **Humans**: neural substrates, trained on embodied experience

Both are **next-token predictors**.

## The Virus Metaphor

Barenholtz explicitly develops the **virus/life-form metaphor** for language [5]:
- Language **replicates** through human hosts
- Language **evolves** to maximize prediction success
- Language **does not care** about truth, only continuation
- Humans are **not users** but **carriers**

This is the **ultimate nemetic inversion**: the pattern (language) is the agent; the human is the vector.
