## Actual Occasions: Events, Not Things

### The Primary Definition

Whitehead defines **actual occasions** (or actual entities) as the **ultimate units of reality**, "drops of experience, complex and interdependent" [1].

Enduring things are **societies of such occasions**, not substances that persist unchanged.

### The IEP Summary

The Internet Encyclopedia of Philosophy summarizes this well:

> "An entity that people commonly think of as a simple concrete object…is in this ontology considered to be a **composite of indefinitely many occasions of experience**." [1]

### The Becoming of Continuity

The IEP emphasizes that **continuity is derived from discrete becomings**:

> He rejects a "continuity of becoming" in favor of the **"becoming of continuity,"** where many atomic occasions "incorporate themselves into the continuity of the universe by feeling the concreteness of what has come before and making that concreteness a part of the occasion's own internal makeup." [1]

This is the **nemetic insight**: pattern emerges from coordination, not from persistence of unit.

## Time and Experience

The Whitehead Encyclopedia explicitly frames the **"primary metaphysical situation"** as:

> "An actual occasion's **subjective experiencing of its causal past**." [2]

It explains how:

> "A human mind is **comprised of a linear series of actual occasions** that are intermingled with the actual occasions comprising a brain," underscoring that persons are **streams of occasions, not enduring substances**. [2]

### Events as Nexus

Religion Online clarifies:

> "An actual occasion is the limiting type of event with only one member," and more generally, **"an event is a nexus of actual occasions."** [3]

This supports the reading of **entities as events of becoming** rather than static units.

## Prehension: Metabolizing the Past

### The Clear Description

The IEP gives a clear description of **prehension**:

> "An actual occasion **'prehends' its world** (relationally takes that world in) by feeling the 'objective data' of past occasions which the new occasion utilizes in its own concrescence." [1]

### The Pattern of Metabolism

The same entry notes:

> "The many occasions of the universe that have already become **contribute their atomic reality to the becoming of a new occasion** ('the many become one'). However, this occasion…now **contributes that reality to the previously achieved realities** of the other occasions ('and increase by one')." [1]

This is **exactly the pattern** where the past is "metabolized" into the present: the new occasion is its way of **taking up and transforming its causal past**.

Whitehead's metaphysics makes **NEMAtic operation the basic structure of reality**:

1. **Everything that is** is a momentary coordination
2. **Inherited pattern** is taken up, transformed, re-expressed
3. **New pattern** becomes datum for future coordinations
4. **No enduring substance**—only stream of occasions
5. **No fixed ground**—only ongoing web of prehensions

This is not a special case or metaphor. This is **ontology**.
