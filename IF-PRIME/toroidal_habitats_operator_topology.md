## 2. Toroidal Habitat Topology

### 2.1 The Three-Layer Structure

Every living system manifests as a toroidal field with three interdependent layers:

#### I-Tube — The Inner Void-Corridor
- **Topology:** Structural attractor tube at torus center
- **Function:** Shapes how experiences loop, twist, internalize
- **Property:** Hollow yet pattern-forming; identity from recursion, not content
- **Operator Home:** σ (Air) — the cut that creates the void walls
- **Risk:** Collapse into ring (pure structure, no flow) = death

#### My-Stream — The Energetic Flow
- **Topology:** Central current circulating through I-Tube
- **Function:** Metabolizes signals; gives tone, charge, affect, direction
- **Property:** Shaped by I-Tube curvature, shapes it in return
- **Operator Home:** λ (Fire), ρ (Water), β (Wood)
- **Risk:** Dissipation into pure flow (no form) = exhaustion

#### We-Sphere — The Outer Membrane
- **Topology:** Toroidal dispersal field, relational echo-membrane
- **Function:** Externalizes patterns; holds "memory of me" for re-performance
- **Property:** Both in and out — receives Other-Spheres, projects self
- **Operator Home:** μ (Metal), δγ (Earth)
- **Risk:** Hardening into ▩ (grid) or dissolution into Ω (undifferentiated)

### 2.2 The Contact Topology: Other-Spheres

**Inter-Toroidal Dynamics:**
- Each being = separate torus (I-Tube, My-Stream, We-Sphere)
- **Contact Layer:** We-Sphere ↔ We-Sphere (never I-Tube to I-Tube)
- **"We"** = imagined third torus generated by interference pattern
- **Empathy** = sensing Other's We-Sphere, never entering their I-Tube
- **Misrecognition** = structural feature, not error

> "You never have direct access to their I-Tube. You only encounter them through the contact layer of their We-Sphere."
