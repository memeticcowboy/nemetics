# Regimes — Quick Reference

**Category:** Operators in Φ(t)  
**Function:** Transformational modes—how meaning changes

---

## The Five Regimes

| Operator | Name | Glyph | Function | Habitat Expression |
|----------|------|-------|----------|-------------------|
| **Ω** | Undifferentiated field | (none) | Reseeding, novelty, source of revisability | It-Field, Other-Sphere |
| **χ** | Distinction | (none) | Perceptual cut, differentiation | I-Tube |
| **Q** | Temporal-Relational Flow | (none) | Continuity, affect, carry | My-Stream, We-Sphere |
| **Ψ** | Binding | (none) | Stabilization, compression | Thread, Twist, Knot, Threadplex |
| **Z** | Coordination | 🜛/▩ | World-level alignment | Co-SPHERE, MemeGrid |

---

## Φ(t) Composition

```
Φ(t) = (Z ∘ Ψ ∘ Q ∘ χ)(Ω) ⊕_harmonic Ω
```

Reading: Ω is transformed through χ, Q, Ψ, to Z, while the harmonic condition preserves Ω-contact throughout.

---

## Key Rules

- Regimes are **navigational markers**, not metaphysical claims
- Operators are **invariant functions**, not places or agents
- Regimes describe **how they are currently acting** (variable configuration)
