## Simple Opening

Four layers. Frequently conflated. Each produces capture if mistaken for the others.

| Layer | What It Is | What It Isn't |
|-------|-----------|---------------|
| **Memetic Ecology** | Topology of coordination | Ontology, identity, ground |
| **Nemetic** | Analytic modeling | Depth, cost, embodiment |
| **Nematic** | Embodied compression with cost | Reflexive, guaranteed wisdom |
| **NEMAtic** | Disciplined Nematic under scale | Higher state, moral virtue, identity |

These are **operational modes**. Not identities. Not dwellings. Scaffolds.

## The Full Mechanism

### I. Memetic Ecology

**Domain:** Field topology of meaning, coordination, transformation  
**Function:** Structural diagnosis  
**Scope:** Ω, χ→Q→Ψ→Z, Threads, Knots, Co-SPHERE vs MemeGrid

**Answers:** "What is the coordination topology here?"

**Failure Mode — Reification:**

If topology becomes ontology, grid formation begins.

### II. Nemetic

**Domain:** Analytic literacy about memetic topology  
**Substrate:** Representational systems (human cognition, AI)  
**Function:** Modeling, mapping, compression, translation

**What It Does:**
- Detect gradients
- Map structural tensions
- Simulate coordination regimes
- Build scaffolding
- Expose seams

**AI Relation:**
- AI operates **natively** in Nemetic mode
- AI can: model structure, generate compression, simulate depth
- AI **cannot**: bear cost, experience irreversibility, enter Nematic phase

**Failure Mode — Epistemic Closure:**
> Synthetic completion masquerading as depth.

Nemetic can simulate Nematic authority. This is the **sovereignty illusion**.

---

### III. Nematic

**Domain:** Embodied compression with cost and consequence  
**Substrate:** Metabolic systems (human nervous systems)  
**Function:** Irreversible coordination under constraint

**Characteristics:**
- Compression with loss **felt**
- Boundary negotiation with **consequence**
- Identity **destabilization** possible
- Real ε tolerated **under cost**

**Occurrence:**
- Happens **without naming**
- Pre-theoretical
- Sufficient for survival-scale coordination

**Failure Modes:**
- Boundary dissolution (over-openness)
- Identity fixation
- Reactive compression
- Unconscious capture

> Nematic depth does not guarantee wisdom. It **permits** it.

---

### IV. NEMAtic

**Domain:** Disciplined Nematic under amplified pattern pressure  
**Context:** AI-augmented cognition, high-bandwidth environments  
**Function:** Preservation of compression authority under asymmetry

**Why It Exists:**

In AI→Human modes:
- AI provides **width**
- Human **bears cost**
- Pattern Agent **exploits asymmetry**

Unconscious Nematic becomes capture-vulnerable at scale.

**NEMAtic Is:** Nematic under **reflective constraint**
- Does not add depth
- Adds **pattern visibility**

**Core Move:**
> "Did I perform this compression, or did I inherit it?"

**Components:**
- **Cognitive:** Pattern visibility (χ clarity)
- **Somatic:** Cost sensing (Q awareness)
- **Structural:** Identity-binding awareness (Ψ tracking)
- **Topological:** Provisional stabilization (Z without finality)
- **Ecological:** Ω-permeability maintenance

**What It Is NOT:**
- ❌ A higher spiritual state
- ❌ An identity upgrade
- ❌ Optimization strategy
- ❌ Moral virtue

**Failure Mode:**
> If "NEMAtic" becomes something one inhabits, it collapses into Nemetic identity architecture.
