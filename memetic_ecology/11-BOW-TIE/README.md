## What Bow-Tie Topology Is

> **Bow-Tie Topology is the unavoidable compression–expansion topology of sensemaking across the stack; elemental regimes bias how it deforms, operators govern how it reverses, and health depends on remembering that the bottleneck is forgetting, not truth.**

At its core, Bow-Tie Topology is **not** a component, agent, or regime. It is a **structural inevitability** of any finite sensemaking system.

> **Bow-tie = the forced topology of meaning under constraint.**

Whenever:
- Inputs exceed capacity
- Action must occur now
- History cannot be fully preserved

The system **must**:
1. **Compress** (forget most distinctions)
2. **Act through a bottleneck** (Now)
3. **Expand creatively** (invent usable output)

This is not optional. Not ideological. **Physics + time + finitude.**
