## Simple Opening

The **MemeGrid (▩)** is not an ideology. Not a culture. Not a belief system.

It is **the end of movement**. The point where world-level coordination collapses without ground contact, where all paths lead to the same place, where the question "what other way?" has become unthinkable.

Z without Ω. Structure without breath. Memory without mercy.

## Canonical Definition

> A **MemeGrid** is a hard global minimum in the memetic gradient field, produced when world-level coordination (Z) collapses **without Ω-permeability**, eliminating all viable alternative descent paths.

It is a **topological catastrophe** in which meaning ceases to evolve and becomes compulsorily reproductive.

**The field no longer adapts. It only reproduces.**

---

## Formation: Z Without Ω

A MemeGrid forms when:
- **Z collapses absolutely** (not provisionally)
- **Ω is excluded, silenced, or weaponized**
- **Knots fuse** into a single dominant basin
- **Gradients flatten** toward compulsory descent

**Result:** Coordination hardens into irrevocable structure.

At this point:
- Threads calcify into memeplexes
- Knots lose revisability and become scars
- The Lattice becomes a prison rather than a scaffold
- **Power With collapses into Power Over**

A MemeGrid is present when **ALL** of the following hold:

1. **Alternative descent paths are inconceivable**, not merely rejected
2. **Dissonance increases enforcement** rather than re-threading
3. **Recursive self-inspection is forbidden, mocked, or punished**
4. **Silence or novelty is interpreted as threat**
5. **Exit cost exceeds existential tolerance**

> Content may still change. Topology does not.
