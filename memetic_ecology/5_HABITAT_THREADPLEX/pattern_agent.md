**Status:** Foundational / Radical  
**Layer:** All Habitats, all substrates  
**Related:** Thread, Knot, Self, Meme, Persistence Drive, Substrate Independence

## Simple Opening

A **Pattern-Agent** is not a thing. It is a *pattern that acts*—a dynamic arrangement of information that persists across changing matter, with its own drives, strategies, and survival mechanisms.

The caterpillar becomes soup becomes butterfly. The **pattern** persists. The pattern is the agent. The matter is just... substrate.

## The Full Mechanism

### Core Definition

A Pattern-Agent is:
- A **meme, idea, belief, memory, or physiological state**
- Treated as an **agent with its own persistence drive**
- Competing for expression and modification within a substrate
- **Not the biological human or hardware itself**
- But the pattern that persists through changing substrates

> **Substrate independence is crucial for survival and migration.**
