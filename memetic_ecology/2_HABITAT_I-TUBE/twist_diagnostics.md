## The Phenomenon

In the I-Tube, Twists form when the reflexive loop catches on itself:

- "I am thinking about myself thinking"
- "I notice that I notice"
- "This is MY experience of having an experience"

Each loop adds torsion. Without ε-space, the torsion tightens into Knot.

---

## Diagnostic Tests

### Twist vs Knot Test
> Can this unwind *without explanation*?
- Yes → Twist (still torsional, still revisable)
- No → Knot (stabilized, requires work to dissolve)

### Self-Referential Torsion Markers
- Infinite regress anxiety ("but who watches the watcher?")
- Identity vertigo ("which 'I' is speaking?")
- Recursive doubt ("am I really feeling this or performing feeling?")
