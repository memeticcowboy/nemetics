## Canonical Description (§3.5)

A **hauntological effect** is produced when:
- Twists stabilize into Knots
- The originating context dissolves
- The force persists without narrative justification
- **ε was not reintroduced**

**Hauntology = force without story.**

---

## The Phenomenology

Haunted systems feel:
- Tense without cause
- Obligated without reason
- Resistant to change without explanation

These are **obsolete compressions** still shaping descent paths.

---

## Structural Production

Hauntological Knots are **structurally—not psychologically—produced**.

They are Twists that were **never allowed to unwind while ε was present**.

---

## Habitat Diagnostic

Hauntological effects are most visible in the **Temporal Flexibility axis** (§2.5):
- **Frozen Habitats** accumulate hauntological Knots
- **Adaptive Habitats** metabolize them through Earth (☷) cycling
