# Local Minimum — Complicated Term

**Status:** Intermediate (between simple and formal)  
**Layer:** Knot (Ψ) — provisional stabilization  
**Related:** Thread, Knot, MemeGrid, revisability, escape cost

---

## Simple Opening

A **local minimum** is where meaning rests because it is *tired*—not because it is *finished*.

Not truth. Not destiny. Just... a place where movement slows because things "fit" well enough, for now.

---

## The Full Mechanism

### Why "Minimum" Is the Right Metaphor

In gradient dynamics, meaning moves by **relaxation**, not decision:
- From ambiguity → coherence
- From intensity → usability  
- From novelty → familiarity

A **minimum** is where:
- Effort drops
- Friction decreases
- Things "fit" better than nearby alternatives

A **local** minimum means:
- It is *not* the lowest possible point
- It is only stable *relative to nearby gradients*
- **Escape is possible if pressure increases**

> A Knot is not where meaning is *true*. It is where meaning becomes **easy enough to reuse**.

---

## What Descends Along a Thread?

Not propositions. **High-dimensional lived experience**:

| Vector | What It Carries |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Phonetic** | Sounds, words, symbols |
| **Semantic** | Associations, meanings |
| **Visceral** | Posture, affect, readiness |

Repeated traversal carves a groove:
- Fewer degrees of freedom required
- Anticipation replaces deliberation
- Response precedes reflection

**That groove is the Knot.**

> A local minimum is not a belief you hold. It is **a path your nervous system, language, and relations already know how to walk**.

---

## Why Knots Feel Like Identity (Even Though They Aren't)

Knots feel sovereign because they sit at the intersection of:
- **Repetition** (memory)
- **Embodiment** (felt continuity)
- **Social mirroring** (recognition)

### Three Illusions

| Illusion | The Feeling | The Structure |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| **Necessity** | "This is just how things are" | Repeated traversal |
| **Ownership** | "This is who I am" | Embodied groove |
| **Authority** | "This must be defended" | Social recognition |

**Structurally, none are true.**

A Knot has:
- **No command power**
- **No interior essence**
- Only persistence through repeated traversal

> The moment a Knot claims authority, it stops being a local minimum and starts becoming a **global attractor**—which is exactly how MemeGrids form.

---

## The Ethical Line: Revisability

### Healthy Local Minimum

- Enables action
- Reduces cognitive load
- Supports coordination
- **Without forbidding alternatives**

You can:
- Stay
- Leave
- Loosen without rupture

### Pathological Local Minimum

- Leaving feels like annihilation
- Questioning feels like betrayal
- Alternatives become inconceivable

This is **over-binding** and **recursive sealing**:
- The Knot is no longer local
- The gradient field flattens
- Z collapses without Ω
- The ecology slides toward **▩ MemeGrid**
