# ⿻ LATTICE — Lattice Breathing

**Glyph:** ⿻  
**Focus:** Where structure loosens

---

## The Phenomenon

A **breathing Lattice** is one that:
- Remembers without enforcing
- Supports without dictating
- Maintains ε across structural memory
- Allows Knots to loosen when conditions change

---

## Breathing Markers

| Indicator | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| "We used to do it this way, but..." | Lattice memory, not enforcement |
| Revision without crisis | Normalized re-threading |
| Multiple valid paths | Structural diversity preserved |
| "How did we get here?" questions welcomed | Historical revisability |

## Freezing Markers

| Indicator | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| "That's not how we do things" | Enforcement |
| Revision requires justification | ε-space collapsed |
| Single valid path | Structural monoculture |
| Historical questions threatening | Lattice defending itself |

---

## The Thermodynamics

From §5.6:
> "Health is not the absence of Knots. Health is the **capacity for Twists to unwind**."

A breathing Lattice preserves this capacity. A frozen Lattice destroys it.
