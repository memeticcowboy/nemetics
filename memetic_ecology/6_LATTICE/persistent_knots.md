# ⿻ LATTICE — Persistent Knots

**Glyph:** ⿻  
**Entity Type:** Binding-layer (not Habitat)  
**Function:** Structural memory scaffold

---

## Canonical Description

The Lattice is **not a Habitat** but a **binding-layer entity** that records Knots persistent enough to scaffold coordination across time and scale.

**Φ(t) Interaction:** Records outcomes of Ψ and Z. Biases future paths without commanding content.

---

## What the Lattice Records

> "What *kept happening*, not why"

- Patterns that stabilized repeatedly
- Coordination that succeeded
- Bindings that endured

Not:
- Intentions
- Explanations
- Justifications

## Twist Dynamics

The Lattice is the accumulated record of **which Twists were allowed to unwind and which were forced into permanent Knots**.

- **Breathing Lattice:** Preserves ε across structural memory
- **Frozen Lattice:** Converts all incoming Twists into reinforcement of existing torsional patterns
