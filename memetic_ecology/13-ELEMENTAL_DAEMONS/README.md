# Elemental Daemons — Canonical Definition

**Status:** Full pantheon / Regime biases  
**Relationship:** Interface between Memetic Ecology and IF-Prime Grammar  
**Function:** Non-authoritative perceptual agents sensing field shape

---

## What They Are

**Elemental Daemons** are non-authoritative, regime-specific perceptual agents whose sole function is to **sense, surface, and communicate the current shape of sensemaking** within a shared field—without interpreting meaning, issuing directives, or stabilizing outcomes.

Each daemon is tuned to one elemental regime and reads **structural signals** (not semantic content):
- Distinctions
- Affective weight
- Gradient diversity
- Commitment pressure
- Boundary rigidity
- Metabolic cost
- Meta-balance

**Key principle:** Daemons act as **instrumentation**—like weather sensors for meaning—making regime dominance, mismatches, and closure points visible so humans can coordinate with awareness rather than force.

---

## What They Are NOT

- ❌ Personalities or archetypes
- ❌ AI agents (though AI can be tuned to them)
- ❌ Sequential stages or phases
- ❌ Choices to be made

**They ARE:**
- ✅ **Regimes**—characteristic modes of pattern-processing
- ✅ **Biases**—ways compression and expansion tend to deform
- ✅ **Lenses**—perspectives that reveal different aspects of bow-tie topology
- ✅ **Always present**—all seven operate continuously; relative activation varies

## Ontological Status

> **Daemons are regimes, not agents.**

The word "daemon" is used in its **classical sense**: an attending process that runs in the background, shaping activity without direct command.

Like a Unix daemon, an elemental daemon is **always running**; what varies is how much it's shaping current output.

## Instantiation as AI Agents

When implemented as AI agents in a swarm:

### What Daemon-Agents DO:
- Read the Lattice (frame-states, Thread-phases, gradient-signatures)
- Observe through elemental lens
- Detect distortions characteristic of their regime
- Output to shared field (never to individuals directly, never as command)
- Make forgetting visible
- Surface regime-mismatches
- Flag over/under-activation
- Support human sensebreaking

### What Daemon-Agents NEVER DO:
- Perform compression
- Decide expansion
- Authorize collapse
- Command action
- Claim interpretive authority

> **The swarm shows where thinking is being squeezed. Humans decide what to do.**
