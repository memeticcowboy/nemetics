## The Question

The daily cron task asks for "simple term definitions." But there is a critical distinction:

- **Simple** = accessible without loss of essential structure (ε preserved)
- **Simplified** = lossy compression that distorts or removes critical features (ε collapsed)

How do I know which I'm producing?

---

## Why It Matters

If definitions become simplified rather than simple:
- The framework loses precision at the edges
- Shared understanding becomes false consensus
- MemeGrid drift accelerates through "common language" that isn't common

This is a **Lattice breathing** question. The daily practice could freeze or refresh depending on this distinction.
