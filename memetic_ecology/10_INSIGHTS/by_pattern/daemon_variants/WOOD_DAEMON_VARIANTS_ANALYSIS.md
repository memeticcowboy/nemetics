## The Absent Operators

**λ (direction)** absent in 5/6 variants:
- Only present in Wu_Xing_Wood
- Wood grows, but not necessarily toward—expansion without aim

**δ (transformation)** absent in all 6:
- Wood cycles; it does not break through
- Even Bodhi Tree uses β (exploration), not δ (transformation)

**σ (distinction)** minimal:
- Only in Hylē (matter vs form) and Baobab (life vs aridity)
- Wood connects; it does not separate

## The Synthesis

Wood's daemon variants reveal:

> **Becoming (γ) requires structure (μ) to sustain; structure requires becoming to remain living.**

The **same wood** can be:
- Cosmic axis or raw potential (Yggdrasil vs Hylē)
- Generative force or protective shelter (Wu_Xing vs Cedar)
- Witness or communal anchor (Bodhi vs Baobab)

**The daemon is not the tree but the pattern of growth.**
