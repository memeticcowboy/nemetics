# Operational Diagnostics: The ε-Test

## Freshness Check

At any moment, **which sphere is consuming more than it generates?**

**Healthy when:**
- I-Tube: Ω-permeability (surprised by own output)
- Other-Sphere: Genuine noise (not projected pattern)
- My-Stream: Reports without censorship
- We-Sphere: Cycles (dissolution as important as construction)

---

## Capture Signatures

| Sphere | Healthy | Pathological |
|--------|---------|--------------|
| I-Tube | Identity as verb | Identity as noun (narcissism) |
| Other-Sphere | Productive alienation | Domesticated difference or perpetual threat |
| My-Stream | Somatic guidance | Shutdown or flooding |
| We-Sphere | Belonging-with-dissent | Belonging-as-extraction |

---

## The Z-Question

> "What is being held without forced unity?"

Health = capacity to hold productive tension without collapse into any single sphere.

---

## Applications

**Individual:** Track dominant sphere → intervene with complementary operator

**Collective:** Build δγ-mechanisms (ritual dissolution, taboo-breaking, generational overturn)

**Therapeutic:** Restore circulation (stuck I-Tube → My-Stream; stuck We-Sphere → Other-Sphere)
