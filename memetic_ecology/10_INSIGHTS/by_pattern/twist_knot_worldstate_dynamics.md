## Lattice Breathing: The Key Mechanism

Daniel's insight:
> "The Lattice could be said to breathe in such a way that Knots might unwind and become Twists, or be free entirely."

This means:
- Lattice remembers *how* (process, context, conditions)
- Not *what* (content, command, obligation)
- History as consultable, not sovereign
- Structure enables loosening, not just records tightening

**Breathing Lattice:**
- Knots can return to Twist status
- Twists can dissipate entirely
- Residual force redistributes (lumemic)

**Frozen Lattice:**
- Knots are permanent
- Twists must tighten
- Residual force accumulates (usurpenic)
