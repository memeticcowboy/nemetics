## Simple Opening

**Ω (Omega)** is not a thing. It is **what allows things to be**.

Not meaning. Not identity. Not unity. Just... raw openness. The hum before the cut. The ground that has no shape because it is what gives shape.

You cannot visit Ω. You cannot own it. You can only **be permeable to it**.
