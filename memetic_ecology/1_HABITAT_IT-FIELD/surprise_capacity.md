## The Question

## Markers of Healthy Surprise

- The body reacts before cognition
- Familiar settings yield novel details
- "I never noticed that before" — frequent
- Sensory input exceeds expectation
- The world feels *larger* than the map of it

---

## Markers of Frozen ᖺ

- "Same as always"
- Perceptual field feels flat, thin, manageable
- Novelty requires effort, doesn't arrive unbidden
- The body has no news
- World and map have merged

---

## Recovery Protocols

From §4.11: Forward repairs. Other-Sphere contact can refresh any Habitat directly.

Practical:
- Change sensory context radically
- Attend to edge-of-awareness phenomena
- Engage pre-linguistic practices (movement, sound, touch)
- Allow boredom to deepen without resolution
