## Simple Opening

The **My-Stream (⤸·⤹)** is not identity. Not narrative. Not memory-as-content.

It is the **temporal-affective propagation field**—the wave-space inside the I-Tube where distinction becomes *felt continuity*. Where cuts become continuity.

**It does not decide. It does not explain. It carries and weights experience over time.**
