## Key Insight

**The pathological flip:** The mode that served you starts serving itself.

- Air cares about distinction-making, not clarity
- Water cares about relational engulfment, not connection
- Fire cares about arrival, not direction
- Wood cares about expansion, not creation
- Earth cares about stability, not care
- Metal cares about control, not protection

**The fix:** Reintegration, not rejection. Add the missing elements; don't kill the dominant one.

## Diagnostic Questions

Don't ask: *What's wrong with me?*

Ask: **Which element is dominating my substrate right now?**

- Am I extracting (Air) when I should be relating (Water)?
- Am I dissolving (Water) when I should be protecting (Metal)?
- Am I burning through (Fire) when I should be maintaining (Earth)?
- Am I colonizing (Wood) when I should be integrating (∮)?
